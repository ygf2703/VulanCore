Privacy Policy - VulanCore

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to VulanCore (the "Application" or the "Service"), a system for managing nonprofits and volunteer teams, operated by Frostig Knowledge Transfer.

Your privacy and the privacy of your volunteers are very important to us. This document explains, in a clear and transparent way, what information may be stored in the system and how that information is used.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

VulanCore is designed to help nonprofits and community initiatives manage their operations efficiently. To provide this functionality, the system allows administrators to enter and manage the following types of information:

  • Volunteer data: contact details and operational information used to assign volunteers to tasks and activities, such as names, availability status, and relevant organizational details.
  • Activity and task management data: information about organizational activities, planned tasks, execution status, and dashboard metrics shown to administrators.

All information entered into the system is used solely to operate the Application, provide your organization with management visibility, and deliver the Service properly.

2. No Sale or Transfer of Information to Third Parties

We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your information:

  • No information, including volunteer lists, tasks, or activity data, is sold, rented, shared, or transferred to external parties or third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other unrelated purpose.
  • The information is available only to you and your organization for your ongoing operational use.

3. Information Security

We use standard and commonly accepted security measures to help protect the information stored in the system against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, we will provide notice on this page or within the Application interface.

5. Contact

For any question, clarification, or request regarding this Privacy Policy or the protection of your data, you may contact us through the official website: vulancore.netlify.app.
